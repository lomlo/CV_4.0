My Story

Back in the day, I studied Biology with Psychology with a focus on Neuroscience.

After studying the theory, I wanted to apply theory to practice. I worked as a Support Worker identifying service users needs by practicing user empathy.

I taught children with learning disabilities as a Teaching Assistant. Every day I would observe and report on behaviour, adjusting activities and methods on a requirements basis.

I volunteered in Kenya and worked with local Savings and Credit organisations to provide evidence based loans for new businesses.

I trained in the fundamentals of coding including Object Oriented Design and Test Driven Development.
