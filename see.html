'See' App Design

An app idea designed and prototyped

Tools used in this project

Sketching

Sketch

Flinto

Context

This is an app concept inspired by photography tours demonstrating microinteractions - an important part of the user experience. The design revolves around users participating in 'Photography Challenges' in small groups. Users are able to socialize, share photos and take part in global photography events.

The design process began with sketching, moved to mockups in Sketch and finally exported to Flinto for animated interactions.
