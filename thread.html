Context

Here I present analysis as a user of an e-commerce clothing company , Thread.com. Frequently it is important to compare the approaches to the user experience design of similar services. Thread.com, an e-commerce clothing company, aims to provide users with a stylist to personalise clothing suggestions. It's interesting to understand how they differentiate themselves from market competitors such as ASOS.
