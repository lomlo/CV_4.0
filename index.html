Mobile First Web Redesign

An opportunity to redesign a restaurant website. It begins with a requirements workshop held with clients to develop insight into user and business requirements. The project progresses to usability testing with Uxpin. Google Analytics key metrics were analyzed to inform design decisions presented with accessible graphs.

Requirements Workshop

The Business Model Canvas was a quick way to gather key words for content and to list key features. The workshop concluded with organizing the features into an MVP.
